    Operational Processes of the Ministry

    01

    Loan Procurement

    Officers Involved:

    • Hon. Minister
    • Chief Director
    • Director, ERMERD
    • Unit Head
    • Schedule Officer

    Processes and Procedure:

    1. Review MDAs project document
    2. MoF writes to proposed financiers with project documents attached
    3. Review draft funding agreement/term sheet submitted by financiers
    4. MoF liaises with MDA to submit joint Cabinet memo for Cabinet decision
    5. Upon Cabinet's approval, MoF liaises with MDAs to submit a Parliamentary memo
    6. Upon Parliamentary approval, the Minister for Finance signs the loan agreement
    7. MoF facilitates the fulfillment of all conditions precedent for loan effectiveness
    02

    Disbursement of Project Loans

    Officers Involved:

    • Hon. Minister/ Hon. Minister of State/ Hon. Deputy Minister
    • Chief Director
    • Director, ERMERD
    • Unit Head
    • Schedule Officer

    Processes and Procedures:

    1. MoF reviews the disbursement request from MDA
    2. Authorizes the financier to make payment to the beneficiary account
    3. Financier advises MoF on the transfer
    03

    Sourcing for Grants

    Officers Involved:

    • Hon. Minister/ Hon. Minister of State/ Hon. Deputy Minister
    • Chief Director
    • Director, ERMERD
    • Unit Head
    • Schedule Officer

    Processes and Procedures:

    1. MoF receives and reviews the MoU
    2. MoF requests concurrence from MDA
    3. MoF signs the MoU
    4. MoF requests CAGD to open an account for receipts of funds
    5. MoF requests for disbursement of funds
    04

    Economic Relations

    Officers Involved:

    • Hon. Minister/ Hon. Minister of State/ Hon. Deputy Minister
    • Chief Director
    • Director, ERMERD
    • Unit Head
    • Schedule Officer

    Processes and Procedure:

    1. MoF writes request letter to hold meeting with Development Partner (DP)
    2. Briefs prepared by MoF for meeting on government priority areas
    3. Meeting hosted to consider strategies and SMTDP
    4. Mutual interest and diplomatic ties strengthened
    5. Report prepared for management consideration
    NB: Loan Disbursement and sourcing for grant is DP specific, but the procedures stated are general steps.
